Commuter Alarm Clock

Commuter Alarm Clock helps you solve the problem of arriving late to work due to unpredictable traffic conditions. It works by regularly checking the traffic while you are sleeping to adjust your wakeup time so that you are never late to wherever you need be.

Through the use of the Google Map API, it can access real time traffic conditions to give you a reliable ETA to your destination.
